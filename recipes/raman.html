Raman Improvment

Take any simple store bought raman package and turn it into something more appetizing and filling. It onlyl takes a few more minutes and some common and easy to prepare ingredients!

Ingredients

  • Package of instant raman
  • Eggs
  • Green onion
  • Spicy mayo
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil

Instructions

  1. Add 2 eggs to boiling water and boil for 8 minutes
  2. Remove eggs and add to ice water to cool
  3. Cook noodles and dehydrated ingredients per instructions on raman (DO NOT ADD FLAVOR PACKAGE)
  4. Strain noodles and pat dry
  5. In medium pan on medium heat add 2 Tbsp of sesame oil
  6. Add noodles and fry for 5-7 minutes
  7. Add 1 Tbsp of soy suace and cook 1-2 minutes
  8. Plate noodles and add your boiled eggs green onion and spicy mayo
  9. Enjoy!
