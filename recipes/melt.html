Anything Melt!

The Anything melt is perfect for anyone looking for a quick hot meal. It is essentially a grilled cheese with anything you can add from the fridge. Just browse around for any vegtables, lunchmeat, condiments or even left overs!

Ingredients

  • Bread of any time
  • Butter or oil
  • Cheese or your liking
  • Vegtables of your choosing
  • Meat of any kind

Instructions

  1. Take 2 slices of bread and apply butter or oil to 1 side of each slice
  2. Take any skillet at medium heat and add 1 slice of bread oil/butter side down
  3. Add any condiments of your choosing
  4. Add your cheese and meat
  5. Add your last slice of bread oil/butter side up
  6. Allow to cook 2-3 minutes and flip for another 2-3 minutes
  7. Plate and enjoy!
